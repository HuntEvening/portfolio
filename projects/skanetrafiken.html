Prototype development

This assignments purpose was to create a prototype that will encourage the user to experience and explore new traveling methods through me changing the public transport planning app, currently used in my local area. This was achieved by implementing an already existing travel suggestions that is being shown on the busses and trains. The prototype was created in Illustrator and Photoshop and later put together as a working prototype in InVision.

Course: user centred prototype development
