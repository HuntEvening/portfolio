Guild website

I got a commission to create a website for a guild so they could recruit more players and display their achievements in World of Warcraft Classic. The project was developed together with two other people of which I had the responsibility to put the website together and make sure it works for both desktop and mobile format. The website was created in Wix since the guild leader who commissioned the work wanted a quick way of hosting the website. Except for the website I was also commissioned to create a logo for the guild to use for their discord and to be used on the website.
