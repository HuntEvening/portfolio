Twitch Profile

I got a commission from a friend to create his stream panels for Twitch as well as create a profile picture and a banner. He needed:

  • panel for food break
  • panel for offline
  • panel for stream starting soon
  • panel for technical difficulties
  • panel for toilet break
  • banner for twitch profile
  • profile picture

The pictures were chosen from his favourite champions from League of legends and the text was worked around the pictures, and their themes were chosen from what panels he needed.
